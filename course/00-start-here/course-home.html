Welcome

This course is designed for researchers who already know their way around ImageJ/Fiji â€” you've thresholded, watershedded, and Analyze-Particles'd your way through thousands of images. Now it's time to meet the tools that are replacing those workflows with something dramatically more powerful.

  • Researchers comfortable with basic image processing (thresholding, filtering, ROI management)
  • Anyone curious about how deep learning applies to their specific imaging data
