Objective

Time: ~45 minutes
Prerequisites: Fiji with StarDist and TrackMate-StarDist plugins installed

Takeaways

  • StarDist as a TrackMate detector dramatically reduces detection errors
  • Better per-frame detection leads to better tracking (fewer broken tracks)
  • The combination requires minimal configuration compared to tuning LoG parameters
  • TrackMate's analysis tools work identically regardless of which detector was used
